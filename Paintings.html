LAURENCE VAN DE PERRE 

I am built up from experiences. I carry them with me, all the time.
Some can take them away. Take them all away, one by one. 
They built up over the years. In layers, in different, connecting chapters.  
Experiences grew around me. Made me grew. 
Heavy ones, ugly ones, dirty ones, damaged ones.
Fragile, beautiful, delicate, valuable ones.
Blurry ones, imaginary ones. Dreams. Or lies.
You may unpeel the layers.
23 oktober 2017 

Berlijn (kunsthochschule Weissensee)
